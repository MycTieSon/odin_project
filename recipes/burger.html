Burger Recipe

Description

An american classic with juicy cow patty with toasted crunchy buns.

Ingredients

  • Patty
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup (Optional)

    • Steps:

      1. Grill patty to desired temp
      2. After heating patty proceed to put it on bottom bun.
      3. Put toppings and condiments of choice like lettuce, pickles, and ketchup
