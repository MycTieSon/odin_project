PBJ Recipe

Description

An classic easy-to-make meal

Steps:

    1. Grab two slices of bread (Any brand)
    2. Grab 1 tsp peanut butter and spread it on one slice
    3. Grab 1 tsp of jelly and spread it on the other slice

    4. Lay one slice with the spread facing down and place it on the other slice that has its spread facing up
